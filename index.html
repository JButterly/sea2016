About SEA 2016

SEA is an international forum for researchers in the area of design, analysis, and experimental evaluation and engineering of algorithms, as well as in various aspects of computational optimization and its applications.

The conference proceedings have been published by Springer in the LNCS (Lecture Notes in Computer Science) series.

Organizers

SEA 2016 is organized by Steklov Institute of Mathematics at St. Petersburg of the Russian Academy of Sciences (PDMI).
